Simple application utilizing the following technologies:

  * haml
  * rss
  * hpricot
  * google search
  * Merb 1.0.1
    * partials
    * form helper

Browse the source at: http://github.com/nanodeath/merb_example_google_rss/tree/master

Also note...there are no specs.  I didn't feel like they were within the scope of this app.
