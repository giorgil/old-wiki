====== Hello World ======

  - Either [[installing_merb_from_edge|install edge Merb]] or [[howto/installation/gems|from gems]]. 
  - Generate a new app with<code>
merb-gen app helloworld
</code> and change into the newly created directory<code>
cd helloworld
</code>
  - Generate a new resource by running:<code>
merb-gen resource person
</code> now it's time to fill in the blanks!
