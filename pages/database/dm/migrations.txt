====== DataMapper Migrations ======

Some additional rake tasks are available [[http://aviewfromafar.net/2008/10/26/adventures-in-merbddland-3-reduce-migration-pain-with-db-migrate-all-rake-tasks|here]]

These are:
  * ''rake db:migrate:all''
  * ''rake db:migrate:all:up[version]''
  * ''rake db:migrate:all:down[version]''

If you find them useful, please feedback to the Merb team and they might be included in the standard tasks.
